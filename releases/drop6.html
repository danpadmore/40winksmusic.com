About the event:

On the 25th of October Drop will be hosting Holland's first "BeatDrop" night during the Amsterdam Dance Event (ADE). BeatDrop is an interactive release party where you, the public, determine which tracks end up on the release and how it will sound. So the public will be calling the shots.

The exclusive tracks will be coming from some of the most innovative producers of this moment, from home and abroad, ranging from well known names to emerging talent. After the successful launch in Antwerp, we are now bringing this concept to Amsterdam! Drop#6 will therefore be a very special night during the ADE. Definitely worth just dropping along.

On the night the producers will be dropping two of their hottest tracks and the track which gets the biggest head nod from the audience will be selected for the release. We will be giving away this exclusive collection of cutting edge tracks in cd format. As if that wasn't enough we have asked these guys to remix each others tracks and so complete the double-CD release. The the release will only be available for the guests of Drop #6.

Beatlovers: Don't miss this one !!
