Description:

'An imaginary soundtrack', produced by the leading beat-makers of the world. Leading artists in the genre of 'Electronic x Hip-Hop' join together for one mission - to create a soundtrack for an imaginary movie 'In the First Place, does Digital Harinezumi dream?' Withouth knowing anything about this imaginary movie, they are asked to write a beat to score it.

Then character sparks and beats converge, a nonsensical movie is brought into live by music. Is this a love story? A Sci-Fi? An Epic? A joyful anime? A spy movie? Or a grand opera? No! It's all of them!
